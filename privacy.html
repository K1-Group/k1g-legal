Xcelerator K1G — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 29, 2026

1. Who we are

K1 Group LLC ("K1"), Grand Prairie, Texas, USA. Contact: privacy@k1group.com.

2. What data we access

Through QuickBooks Online APIs we access: Vendor records, Account and Class lists, Bill and BillPayment records, and Company metadata (realmId, company name) for the QBO company you authorize.

3. Why we access it

Solely to create and update Bills in your QBO company based on invoices submitted to your accounts-payable inbox.

4. What we do not do

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing. We do not use your data to train external AI models outside the App's processing pipeline.

5. Storage

OAuth refresh tokens are stored encrypted at rest in Microsoft Azure Key Vault (East US 2). Operational logs (without PII beyond vendor name, amount, and date) are retained for 90 days, then deleted.

6. Sub-processors

Microsoft Azure (hosting, Key Vault, Functions); Intuit (QuickBooks Online APIs).

7. Security

TLS 1.2+ in transit; AES-256 at rest; least-privilege Managed Identity; access limited to authorized K1 personnel with multi-factor authentication.

8. User rights

You may disconnect at any time via QBO » Apps » Connected Apps. Upon disconnect, refresh tokens are revoked and deleted within 24 hours.

9. Breach notification

We will notify affected customers within 72 hours of a confirmed breach.

10. Changes

Updates will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
